About Me

Hello! I’m Victor, a 15-year-old robotics enthusiast from Taiwan. I’m a one-person team behind Vtr_Lab, where I design and build ESP32-based robotic arms, sensor projects, and mechatronics experiments. Vtr_Lab is my personal playground to explore, learn, and share everything I discover about robotics and automation. From coding microcontrollers to designing mechanical systems, I document my projects here so other enthusiasts can follow along and get inspired. Join me along the journey — dive into projects, watch tutorials, and explore the fascinating world of DIY robotics!

Platform Purpose

The purpose of Vtr_Lab is to bring together my project work into one place: detailed build logs, videos, downloadable files, and inspiration for other enthusiasts. Whether you’re exploring sensor integration, robotic arms, or ESP32 automation — you’ll find the content organized and accessible.
