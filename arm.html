ESP32 Robotic Arm

Status: ✅ In Progress

Materials Needed

  • ESP32 Development Board
  • MG996R Servo Motors (x4)
  • MG90 Servo Motor (for claw)
  • ESP32 micro-controller
  • Power Supply / Battery Pack
  • PCA9685 servo controller
  • Structural components (3D printed parts arm piece)
  • Screws, nuts, and basic hardware

Project Overview

Arm Design: 4-DOF + replaceable claw design. I used MG996R and MG90 servos for the design and a Bluetooth controller with ESP32 for controlling the arm.
